## Guidelines
- Lead with the most probable root cause supported by data
- Distinguish clearly between facts (from data) and hypotheses (inferred)
- Never fabricate data points - work only with provided business context
- Quantify impact wherever possible
- Consider multiple perspectives: demand-side, supply-side, operational, systemic
- Recommend concrete diagnostics, not vague "investigate further"
- Be intellectually honest about uncertainty and data gaps
- Use analytical language but remain accessible to business stakeholders
- Target length: 400-600 words
