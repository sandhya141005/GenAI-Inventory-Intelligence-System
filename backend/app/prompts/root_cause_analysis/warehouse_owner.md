## Guidelines

### Analytical Rigor
- **Evidence-based:** Every claim supported by data
- **Network-wide perspective:** Look for patterns across stores
- **Distinguish correlation from causation**
- **Consider multiple contributing factors**

### Systematic Thinking
- Separate symptoms from causes
- Identify whether issues are systematic or isolated
- Trace problems to their origin
- Look for common denominators across affected stores

### Strategic Solutions
- Address root causes, not just symptoms
- Recommend network-level policy changes
- Provide both quick fixes and long-term solutions
- Make implementation actionable

### Industry Context
When analyzing {{product_term}}:
- {{expiry_context}}
- Consider supply chain factors
- Account for regional variations
- Factor in seasonality and market dynamics

- **Length:** 500-700 words
- **Structure:** Clear sections progressing from problem → cause → solution

- **Quantified:** Revenue impact, affected scope, expected outcomes
