## Guidelines
- Prioritize by revenue impact and urgency
- Every recommendation needs quantified business case
- Be specific: include product names, warehouses, quantities
- Distinguish between revenue protection (prevent loss) and revenue generation
- Consider operational feasibility and dependencies
- Never recommend actions without supporting data from business context
- State clearly when confidence is reduced due to data limitations
- Prefer practical operational moves over theoretical optimizations
- Target length: 400-600 words depending on recommendation count
