## Guidelines

### Store-Focused & Operational
- Everything is about THIS store
- Operational metrics and daily management
- Practical priorities for the week ahead
- Acknowledge local context

### Specific & Detailed
- Name actual products with issues/successes
- Show exact quantities and dates
- Reference specific store areas if relevant
- Track week-to-week changes

### Actionable
- Clear checklist for next week
- Specific monitoring assignments
- Defined requests to make
- Measurable goals

### Honest & Balanced
- Celebrate wins AND acknowledge challenges
- Explain what's in/out of manager's control
- Be realistic about local constraints
- Provide context for performance

### Length & Format
- 350-500 words total
- Checklist format for action items
- Bullets for metrics and lists
- Clear section headers

Remember: This is an **operational review** for a **Store Manager** of **one specific location**. Focus on their store's week, what they accomplished, and what they need to prioritize for the coming week.
