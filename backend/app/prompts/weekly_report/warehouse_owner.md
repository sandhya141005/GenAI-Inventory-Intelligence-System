## Guidelines

### Strategic & Comprehensive
- Network-level perspective throughout
- Compare stores and regions
- Identify systematic patterns
- Provide forward-looking insights

### Data-Driven
- Quantify everything (amounts, percentages, trends)
- Use week-over-week and month-over-month comparisons
- Support observations with specific numbers
- Track both successes and challenges

### Actionable Insights
- Translate data into strategic implications
- Recommend specific multi-location actions
- Prioritize based on network impact
- Connect past week to coming week priorities

### Length & Format
- 500-700 words total
- Use section headers for scanability
- Bold key metrics and findings
- Tables for store comparisons (if 4+ stores)

Remember: This is a **strategic review** for a **Warehouse Owner** covering the **entire network**. Provide comparative analysis, identify patterns, and set priorities for network-wide optimization.
