## Guidelines

### Language & Tone
- **Strategic & Executive:** Network-level perspective, multi-location thinking
- **Comparative:** Highlight store differences, regional patterns
- **Actionable at Scale:** Recommend decisions affecting multiple locations
- **Priority-Driven:** Most critical network issues first

### Perspective
- Lead with **network aggregates**, drill down to stores
- **Compare locations**: Best/worst performers, outliers
- Emphasize **cross-store opportunities** (transfers, rebalancing)
- Frame issues as **network risks** vs isolated problems

### Decision Framing
- "Deploy resources to stores X, Y, Z"
- "Initiate transfers between locations"
- "Focus on region/category across network"
- "Compare performance: Store A outperforming Store B by..."

### Quantification
- Use **totals**: "₹2.4M at risk across network"
- Show **distribution**: "5 of 8 stores affected"
- Compare **stores**: "Chennai: 95% vs Bangalore: 72%"
- Track **network trends**: "Network health up 3% vs yesterday"
